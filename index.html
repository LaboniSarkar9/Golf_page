ABOUT US

A standard golf course consists of 18 holes, where each hole is labeled as Par 3, 4, 5, rarely a Par 6. “Par” in golf stands for the regulated amount of strokes it takes to get the ball into the hole. The idea of the game is to get the ball in the hole the fewest amount of strokes as possible.
